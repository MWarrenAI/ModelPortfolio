Concept Design


To begin with Mark Warren and Michael Alcock decided on a basic MOSCOW to get a general gist of what we expected to achieve from our overall project.


Requirement Document for Head


Must


  • Construct a physical concept of the head.
  • Construct a separate manakin head for structure.
  • Construct skin from scan using a Design tool.
  • Scanner for the skin.

Should


  • Extend the design to move the Hinges.
  • Construct Hinges.

Could


  • Construct Eyes.
  • Hair.
  • Additional Motors to control eyes.
  • Speaker for sounds.
  • Colour Scanner.
  • Include a series of Motors to control hinges.

Won't


  • Be for commercial use.
  • Fly.
  • Use Machine Learning to create conversations.
